# Goals of the website transition project **Agency goals** * Improve some of our publishing processes in the transition to "digital-first" * Preserve or improve our perception as a trusted resource for the state **Website goals** * Make it easier to find content on the site * Sell our resources better * Make the site mobile-friendly * Use analytics/metrics to determine what content is most popular * Showcase fresh content on website, social media, other channels (email list?) * Reconsider site structure / arrangement of materials
